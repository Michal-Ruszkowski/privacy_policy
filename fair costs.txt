Privacy Policy for "Fair Costs" Application

Effective Date: 13.03.2024

Fair Costs ("us", "we", or "our") operates the "Fair Costs" mobile application (hereinafter referred to as the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us.
Information Collection And Use

The "Fair Costs" application does not collect any personally identifiable information. The only data collected and stored on the user's device includes:
    Names of group members
    Group name
    Currency used
    Expense details (amount, payer, participants)
    Date of entry of expenses into the application

Use of Data
The data collected is solely used within the "Fair Costs" application for the following purposes:
    To provide and maintain the Service
    To calculate expenses and debts within the group
    To allow users to manage and track shared expenses
    To enhance user experience by providing personalized features

Data Storage
All data entered into the "Fair Costs" application is stored locally on the user's device. We do not store any data on external servers or databases.

Security of Data
The security of your data is important to us. We take reasonable precautions to protect the information collected through the Service. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
